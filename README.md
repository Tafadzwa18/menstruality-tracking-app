# menstruality_tracking_app
*A calm, daily menstrual wellbeing tracker built with Flutter*

## Overview

A lightweight menstrual wellbeing tracker designed to help users understand their body patterns through simple daily check-ins. Instead of focusing only on period prediction, The App helps users track **mood, energy levels, and symptoms** and visualize how these change across different phases of the menstrual cycle.

The app is designed with a **soft, calming interface** using sage green and pink tones to create a supportive and non-clinical user experience.

The MVP focuses on **fast daily logging, privacy-friendly local storage, and clear insights** so users can recognize patterns in their health and wellbeing.

---

## Problem

Many menstrual tracking apps focus primarily on:

- Period prediction
- Fertility tracking

While these are useful, they often overlook **daily wellbeing patterns** such as mood, fatigue, or recurring symptoms.

The app aims to help users answer questions like:

- Why do I feel more energetic during certain days of my cycle?
- When do my symptoms usually appear?
- Are my mood patterns linked to my cycle phase?

By making daily tracking simple, The App helps users build **long-term self-awareness about their health.**
